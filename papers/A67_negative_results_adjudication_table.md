- **UNDERPOWERED** — abandoned or blocked before decisive evidence existed. *Exclusion appendix only.*
- **REVERSED** — a recorded dead end whose blocking premise was later refuted; the failure record
  itself was corrected. *Enters the paper as the falsifiability case study.*
- **OUT-OF-SCOPE** — not a computational-lexicography method claim (dead external hosts, live
  unruled contradictions, adjudications of external critiques).
