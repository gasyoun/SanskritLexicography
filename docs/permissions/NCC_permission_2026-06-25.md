## ✅ Licensing clarified — no contradiction (MG re-query, 03-07-2026)

The original email said both "same terms as CDSL" and "non-commercial only",
which first read as inconsistent (CDSL is CC BY-SA 4.0, whose ShareAlike forbids
adding NC downstream). A follow-up query to Gluckman resolved it: **"same terms
as CDSL" referred to the *attribution style*, not the literal CC BY-SA 4.0
license.** The operative terms are therefore unambiguous:

- **License: CC BY-NC 4.0.** Non-commercial is the real condition; the original
  email explicitly imposed **no share-alike** (nor bulk-download embargo /
  review-before-publish), so it is BY-**NC**, not BY-NC-SA.
- **Attribution: CDSL-style**, crediting the NCC source and Gluckman.

**Practical rule (not a blocker).** ACC (CC BY-SA 4.0) and NCC (CC BY-NC 4.0) are
different licenses, so the ACC×NCC works asset is **dual-licensed**: the ACC spine
and ACC-only entries stay BY-SA; NCC bodies/overlay carry BY-NC, tagged per-field
(or NCC shipped as its own BY-NC asset). NCC content is never relabeled BY-SA and
BY-SA data is never restricted to NC. Ordinary dual-license packaging.
