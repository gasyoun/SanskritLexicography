`_as~~h1_02_sama_di`


**intro)** *samadhi*
- **EN:** *samadhi*

**1)** 1) einen Sitz (acc.) mit einem Andern zugleich einnehmen: *vīrāsanairdhyānajuṣāmṛṣīṇāmamī samadhyāsitavedimadhyāḥ . nivātaniṣkampatayā vibhānti yogādhirūḍhā iva śākhino 'pi ..*
[RAGH. 13,52.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/raghuvamsa/app1?13,52)
- **EN:** 1) "to take a seat" (acc.) "together with another": *vīrāsanairdhyānajuṣāmṛṣīṇāmamī samadhyāsitavedimadhyāḥ . nivātaniṣkampatayā vibhānti yogādhirūḍhā iva śākhino 'pi ..*
[RAGH. 13,52.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/raghuvamsa/app1?13,52)

**2)** 2) bewohmen: *kiṣkindhyāṃ yaḥ samadhyāste guhām*
[R. 6,4,52.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/ramayanagorr/?6,4,52) — desid. einen Platz einnehmen wollen: *samadhyāsisiṣāṃ cakre virūpākṣaḥ purodaram* (d. i. *puramadhyam*)
[BHAṬṬ. 14,16.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/bhattikavya/app1?14,16)
- **EN:** 2) "to inhabit": *kiṣkindhyāṃ yaḥ samadhyāste guhām*
[R. 6,4,52.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/ramayanagorr/?6,4,52) — desid. "to wish to take a place": *samadhyāsisiṣāṃ cakre virūpākṣaḥ purodaram* (d. i. *puramadhyam*)
[BHAṬṬ. 14,16.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/bhattikavya/app1?14,16)
