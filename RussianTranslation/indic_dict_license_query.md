Draft to send to the indic-dict maintainers (Vishvas Vasuki / `@vvasuki`), e.g. as a

**Subject:** License + provenance of the Indic-gloss dictionaries (apte-hi, vedic-rituals-hi, shabdArtha_kaustubha, samskritam-tamizham)

Hello,

Thank you for stardict-sanskrit — it's a valuable consolidation. I'm working on the
Cologne Digital Sanskrit Dictionaries project (`sanskrit-lexicon`), building a
Sanskrit→Russian reference edition of the Petersburg dictionary. We'd like to use a few
of your **`sa-head/other-indic-entries`** dictionaries as an independent *sense-level*
cross-check (never as redistributed translation content), but I can't find license terms.

Could you clarify, for these four specifically:

1. [`apte-hi`](https://github.com/indic-dict/stardict-sanskrit/tree/master/sa-head/other-indic-entries/apte-hi)
   — Apte rendered into Hindi. What is its **upstream source** (which Hindi Apte
   edition / digitization) and under what **license** is it redistributed here?
2. [`vedic-rituals-hi`](https://github.com/indic-dict/stardict-sanskrit/tree/master/sa-head/other-indic-entries/vedic-rituals-hi)
   — source and license of the Vedic-ritual Hindi glossary?
3. [`shabdArtha_kaustubha`](https://github.com/indic-dict/stardict-sanskrit/tree/master/sa-head/other-indic-entries/shabdArtha_kaustubha)
   — source and license? (The `bookname` tags it `sa-sa` but the glosses are Kannada —
   is that intended?)
4. [`samskritam-tamizham_dictionary`](https://github.com/indic-dict/stardict-sanskrit/tree/master/sa-head/other-indic-entries/samskritam-tamizham_dictionary)
   — this credits Visalakshi Ramani's blog. Has she granted permission to
   redistribute, and what attribution / terms does she require?

For context on intended use: we would join on the SLP1 headword key and use a gloss only
to corroborate *which sense is primary* on a card — a soft, non-blocking annotation
alongside our Sanskrit→Russian dictionaries. We would credit each source per its terms.

Separately: is there a repo-wide or per-dictionary `LICENSE` we've missed? The repository
currently reports no SPDX license and the `.babylon` headers carry only `#bookname`.

Thanks very much,
Mārcis Gasūns — Cologne Digital Sanskrit Dictionaries (`sanskrit-lexicon`)
