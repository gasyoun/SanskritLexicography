## prativah

`vah~~h0_35_prati`


**1)** — *prati* entgegenführen: *suyugvahanti prati vāmṛtena*
[ṚV. 3,58,2.] *ājyagandhaṃ prativahanmārutaḥ surabhirvavau*
[HARIV. 13729.] — *pratyasya vaha dyubhiḥ*
[TS. 1,5,3,1] ist aus [VS. 3,8] entstellt.

- **RU:** — *prati* вести навстречу: *suyugvahanti prati vāmṛtena*
[ṚV. 3,58,2.] *ājyagandhaṃ prativahanmārutaḥ surabhirvavau*
[HARIV. 13729.] — *pratyasya vaha dyubhiḥ*
[TS. 1,5,3,1] искажено из [VS. 3,8].
- **EN:** *prati* to lead towards, lead to meet: *suyugvahanti prati vāmṛtena* [ṚV. 3,58,2.] *ājyagandhaṃ prativahanmārutaḥ surabhirvavau* [HARIV. 13729.] — *pratyasya vaha dyubhiḥ* [TS. 1,5,3,1] is corrupted from [VS. 3,8].

**2)** — Vgl. *prativāha, pratīvāha, prativoḍhavya* . — caus. hinführen im Fliessen, hinschwemmen: *kimarthaṃ ca saricchreṣṭhā tamṛṣiṃ pratyavāhayat*
[MBH. 9,2358.]

- **RU:** — Vgl. *prativāha, pratīvāha, prativoḍhavya* . — caus. нести в потоке, смывать в направлении (к чему-либо): *kimarthaṃ ca saricchreṣṭhā tamṛṣiṃ pratyavāhayat*
[MBH. 9,2358.]
- **EN:** Vgl. *prativāha, pratīvāha, prativoḍhavya*. — caus. to carry along in flowing, wash along: *kimarthaṃ ca saricchreṣṭhā tamṛṣiṃ pratyavāhayat* [MBH. 9,2358.]
