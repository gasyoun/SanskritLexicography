[BHĀG. P. 10,89,46.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/bhagp_bom/app1/?10,89,46)
 1) to know, *iti samyaṅmayā jñātaṃ prabhurjānātyataḥ param* so v. a. "the prince knows what he now has to do",
[KATHĀS. 103,81.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/kss/index.html?103,81) *jāne mahāṭavīṃ kāṃcitpraviṣṭā nikhilā vayam* "I know, I remember that"
[69,34.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/kss/index.html?69,34) *ka evaṃ jānīte* with a following fut. "who knows whether not"
[Spr. 3573.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/boesp1/app1/?3573) *naitā jānanti pitaraṃ na kulaṃ na ca mātaram* "they know nothing of their father" u. s. w. so v. a. "they pay no heed to their father u. s. w."
[1646.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/boesp1/app1/?1646) "to remember", with gen.: *hṛdayaṃ tatra jānāti kartuścaiva kṛtasya ca*
[MBH. 12,5169.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/mbhcalc?12.5169) "to assume, to presuppose as known"
[Spr. 2491.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/boesp1/app1/?2491) [5227.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/boesp1/app1/?5227) "to hold to be, to regard as": *khalagiraṃ jānāti satyām*
[2858.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/boesp1/app1/?2858) *svajanaiḥ svātmavajjanturjñāyate guṇavānparaiḥ*
[3324.](https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/boesp1/app1/?3324) *jajñe* pass.
