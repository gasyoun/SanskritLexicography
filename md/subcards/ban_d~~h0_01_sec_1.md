**1)** — caus. *bandhayati*

[KAUŚ. 69.] *aśvam*
[ŚAT. BR. 13,5,4,4.] *bandhayiṣyati vā pāśairatha vāsmānvadhiṣyati*

- **RU:** — caus. *bandhayati*
 1) «велеть связать — посадить в заключение»: *gām*
[KAUŚ. 69.] *aśvam*
[ŚAT. BR. 13,5,4,4.] *bandhayiṣyati vā pāśairatha vāsmānvadhiṣyati*
[R. 2,84,4.]
[KATHĀS. 49,105.]
[RĀJA-TAR. 6,330.] *śatena bandhitaḥ* «долг в сто [единиц] посадил его в тюрьму»
[P. 2,3,24], Sch.
Согласно [DHĀTUP. 32,14], *bandhayati* и *bādhayati* означают «связывать вместе»; vgl. *bandhayitar*.
- **EN:** — caus. *bandhayati*
 1) to have (someone) bound —, have (someone) put in captivity: *gām*
[KAUŚ. 69.] *aśvam*
[ŚAT. BR. 13,5,4,4.] *bandhayiṣyati vā pāśairatha vāsmānvadhiṣyati*
[R. 2,84,4.]
[KATHĀS. 49,105.]
[RĀJA-TAR. 6,330.] *śatena bandhitaḥ* "a debt of a hundred has brought him into prison"
[P. 2,3,24], Sch.
According to [DHĀTUP. 32,14], *bandhayati* and *bādhayati* mean "to bind together"; vgl. *bandhayitar*.
