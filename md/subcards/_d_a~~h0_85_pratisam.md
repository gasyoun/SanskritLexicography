**1)** — *pratisam*
 1) wieder zusammensetzen: *yajñam*
[ŚAT. BR. 12,6,1,2.] [37.] *yadyātmānameva pratisaṃdhatte* wohl sich sammeln

 1) to put together again, to reassemble: *yajñam*
[ŚAT. BR. 12,6,1,2.] [37.] *yadyātmānameva pratisaṃdhatte* probably to collect oneself
[PRAB. 99,14], v. l.

**2)** — 2) daraufsetzen, befestigen

[MBH. 6,3313.] [7,4841.] *paśyataḥ pratisaṃdhāya vidhyataḥ savyasācinaḥ*

- **EN:** — 2) to set upon, to fasten

[MBH. 6,3313.] [7,4841.] *paśyataḥ pratisaṃdhāya vidhyataḥ savyasācinaḥ*
[4,2081.]
