`_d_a~~h0_82_sama_bisam`

[MBH. 3,10452.]
- **EN:** — *samabhisam*
 1) to insert into: *pradeśinīṃ tato 'syāsye śakraḥ samabhisaṃdadhe*
[MBH. 3,10452.]

[MBH. 3,12714.] trachten nach: *yotsye 'haṃ mātulenādya kṣatradharmeṇa ṣārthivāḥ . svayaṃ samabhisaṃdhāya vijayāyetarāya ca ..* fest entschlossen zu siegen oder zu unterliegen
[9,818.]
- **EN:** — 2) to intend: *tapaḥ samabhisaṃdhāya vanamevānvapadyata*
[MBH. 3,12714.] to strive after: *yotsye 'haṃ mātulenādya kṣatradharmeṇa ṣārthivāḥ . svayaṃ samabhisaṃdhāya vijayāyetarāya ca ..* firmly resolved to conquer or to succumb
[9,818.]
