## dhā

`_d_a~~h0_78_atisam`


**1)** — *atisam*
 1) Jmd anführen, betrügen: *kiṃ punarimāmatisaṃdhāya labhyate*
[ŚĀK. 69,23.] *tvayā candramasā ca viśvasanīyābhyāmatisaṃdhīyate kāmijanasārthaḥ*
[32.] [5.] An beiden Stellen hat die v. l. *abhisaṃ˚* .
- **EN:** — *atisam*
 1) to lead someone on, deceive, cheat: *kiṃ punarimāmatisaṃdhāya labhyate*
[ŚĀK. 69,23.] *tvayā candramasā ca viśvasanīyābhyāmatisaṃdhīyate kāmijanasārthaḥ*
[32.] [5.] In both passages the v. l. *abhisaṃ˚*.

**2)** — 2) einen Gegenstand anführen. zu kurz kommen lassen so v. a. ihn nicht in seiner wahren Bedeutung hervortreten lassen, beeinträchtigen: *mūladvāraṃ nānyairdvārairatisaṃdadhīta rūparddhyām . ghaṭaphalapattrapramathādibhiśca tanmaṅgalaiścinuyāt ..*
[VARĀH. BṚH. S. 52,82.]
- **EN:** — 2) to misrepresent an object, to cause it to fall short, thus v. a. not to let it emerge in its true significance, to detract from it: *mūladvāraṃ nānyairdvārairatisaṃdadhīta rūparddhyām . ghaṭaphalapattrapramathādibhiśca tanmaṅgalaiścinuyāt ..*
[VARĀH. BṚH. S. 52,82.]

**denom-note)** — Vgl. *atisaṃdhāna* und *atisaṃdhita* u. *saṃdhay* mit *ati* .
- **EN:** — Vgl. *atisaṃdhāna* and *atisaṃdhita* u. *saṃdhay* with *ati*.
