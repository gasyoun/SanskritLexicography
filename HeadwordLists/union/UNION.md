**Caveats.** The fold is deliberately split: only the unambiguous **`-inńę`‚Üí`-in`** (gender-confirmed) is applied automatically; `-ńĀ`/`-ńę` are left as **candidates** because a feminine `-ńĀ` noun often shares a stem with an unrelated masculine `-a` noun. Feminines without a `<lex>` (dicts lacking gender tags) or without an attested base stay as their own headword. Homograph numbering (`<h>`) is collapsed ‚ÄĒ the union key is the bare lemma. Covers all 15 dicts with a csl-orig `<k1>` (PD has none); gender is richest in MW/AP and sparse elsewhere (BUR has no `<lex>`), so folds are MW/AP-driven.
